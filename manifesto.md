The process
1. Do it - see what happens - then think about what happened
2. If in doubt - again, do it and see
3. Don't get lost in the work - rest and reflect
4. Reflect to innovate
5. Copy, combine, rearrange, remix
6. Don't worry about being unoriginal - worry about not making it - put things out there and the rest will emerge
7. Re-invent the wheel
8. You can only improve what's already there

The values
1. Be truthful with your work
2. Be open with your intentions
3. Be mindful of yourself
4. Be mindful of others

The insights
1. Drawing boundaries creates space
